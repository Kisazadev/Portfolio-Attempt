What inspires me

Even after all the troubles I went through learning basic computer science concepts through python, c++, javascript, and the wonders of installing things through your command line on a non-unix system, why do I still want to be a developer instead of pursuing the lucrative career as a classical guitarist? Here's why.

You never stop learning

My personality requires that I'm constantly moving forward and never be stagnating. Languages and frameworks will come and go, but I'm here to stay and learn them all.

Technology is awesome

The amount of cool things that you can do on just the command line is amazing. Our entire lives revolve around technology and I love tinkering with it. I can't stay away.

Community

It seems that most people in the tech scene are extremely passionate about what they do. So much is open source and we are all here to help eachother out. I love this. I want to be a part of this.
